Solace Weather (SwiftUI, Combine)

Stay a step ahead of the weather. Blend the weather with the madness that is your calendar and find solace in it. This slick, new weather app takes advantage of the new SwiftUI framework and Combine library by Apple for a reactive and beautiful way to track the weather outside.

Blockify for Discord (C#, Windows Forms)

Convey small text into big and bold characters for people to see on Discord. This neat companion tool simplifies the process of converting plaintext to regional indicators.

Foray (SwiftUI, Combine)

An interest-driven social networking application submitted to HackGSU 2020, and was presented to Microsoft, Diversified, and State Farm. It was a valuable learning experience integrating the native Swift codebase at the frontend, to our Azure-based SQL database.
